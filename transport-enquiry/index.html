About Us

At WaveX, we believe that transport information should be accessible, accurate, and easy to use. Our mission is to revolutionize the way people interact with public and private transport systems. We aggregate real-time data and timetables across multiple modes of transport—buses, trains, flights—and present it through a clean, intuitive interface. Whether you're commuting daily or planning a long journey, WaveX is designed to be your trusted partner.

Our dedicated team is constantly enhancing the platform to deliver precision, reliability, and user satisfaction. With smart tracking, optimized routes, and detailed fare breakdowns, our system empowers travelers to plan better and reduce travel stress. We're committed to making your journey smarter, smoother, and more informed—every single time you travel.
